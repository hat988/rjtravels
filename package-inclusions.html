What's Included in Your Package

Every detail is thoughtfully arranged to ensure a seamless and comfortable journey.

Air Tickets

Round-trip economy class airfare on premium airlines, with convenient flight timings to minimize your travel fatigue.

Visa Processing

Complete assistance with visa application and processing, ensuring all documentation is handled correctly and efficiently.

Star Hotels

Accommodation in certified 4 or 5-star hotels, located within walking distance of the Haramain for your convenience.

A/C Transport

Travel comfortably in modern, air-conditioned buses for airport transfers, inter-city travel, and Ziyarat tours.

Three Daily Meals

Enjoy delicious and hygienic buffet-style meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, featuring a variety of cuisines.

Laundry Service

Convenient laundry service is included, ensuring your ihram and other clothing remain clean throughout your stay.

Travel Bags

Receive complimentary, durable travel bags and pouches to help you stay organized on your sacred journey.

5L Zam Zam

As a blessed gift, each pilgrim will receive 5 litres of Zam Zam water upon their return journey home.

Expert Guide / A Mu'allim

Travel with a knowledgeable Mu’allim who will guide you at every step, ensuring your pilgrimage is smooth and spiritually fulfilling.

Complimentary Ziyarat

Take part in complimentary guided Ziyarats to significant holy sites, enriching your pilgrimage experience with deeper spiritual insights.

Riyal Exchange

Convenient Riyal exchange services provided, ensuring you have local currency on hand for a hassle-free pilgrimage experience.

Haji Card

Receive a special Haji Card containing your essential details, designed for easy identification and added safety throughout your journey.
